Hitomi Hirayama

About me

My full CV is available here.

I am interested in semantics and pragmatics in Japanese. It's really fascinating to investigate how rich contextual information is expressed by this language and how it is understood by the addressee. I am also very interested in carrying out experiments to understand how people actually use languages. I have been working on Japanese since I started my PhD program at University of California, Santa Cruz. Before that, I was working on Italian nominals during my BA and MA program at University of Tokyo.
